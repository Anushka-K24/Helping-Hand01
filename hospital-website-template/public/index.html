About Us

Best Elderly Care For Your Family

Helping Hand is a Pune based bureau to serve customers for their caretaking needs. Considering the ever-increasing need of caretakers for the people across all the ages, we at Helping Hand provide our services to the Elderly, Infants and to anyone who is need of care taking services. We cater our Services according to the needs of the Customers. The prime focus is to give the best care to our customers in the form of reliability, cleanliness and love which helps the patient in recovering as fast as possible. We serve all types of patients including those suffering from Chronic illnesses, injuries, disorders, etc. Our Belief is a “Strong Customer Satisfaction” to a vast base of Customers which continues to grow day by day. We at “Helping Hand” have only Experienced Personnel so that only people who served the Patients in the past with considerable period are employed by us.
